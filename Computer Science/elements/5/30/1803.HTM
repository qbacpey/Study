[...] can be used for different kinds of success.

If you [...] upon someone, you have influence on them.

If you [...] over someone, you win.

Say you’re a big fan of a losing team. You might cheer, “Despite all odds, we will [...]!”

To use [...], there has to be some kind of contest or conflict. That could be internal, like when you have a pile of homework to do, but your desire to play video games [...] over your discipline.
