As with fork, the word wait is now a bit ambiguous. It is used for pausing the current UNIX process to wait for another process to complete; it is also used in thread synchronization, for waiting on a condition variable. To disambiguate, we will always use the term “UNIX wait” to refer to UNIX’s wait system call.

UNIX 中的 wait 具有两种不同的含义：

1. 如果在进程相关的上下文中谈到 wait（以及 fork），那么 wait 所指代的就是暂停当前进程以等待另一个进程完成（wait for another process to complete）；

2. 如果是在线程相关的上下文中谈到 wait（具体来说是线程同步相关事宜）那么就不是等待某个线程完成（wait for another thread to complete）而是等待某个条件变量（waiting on a condition variable）；

眼下，对于进程相关的 wait（即所谓的 wait 系统调用），书中会将其称为 UNIX wait

Oddly, waiting for a thread to complete is called “thread join”, even though it is most analogous to UNIX wait.

特别的，谈及等待某个线程完成（waiting for a thread to complete）的行为时，我们会使用“thread join”这个词

Windows is simpler, with a single function called “WaitForSingleObject” that can wait for process completion, thread completion, or on a condition variable

至于在 Windows 中，WaitForSingleObject 能同时用于等待进程完成、等待线程完成以及等待条件变量
