1.N-COUNT 可数名词龙头;开关 A faucet is a device that controls the flow of a liquid or gas from a pipe or container. Sinks and baths have faucets attached to them.

【语域标签】：AM 美

She turned off the faucet and dried her hands.

她关掉水龙头，把手擦干（美）

She turned off the tap and dried her hands.

她关掉水龙头，把手擦干（英）

in BRIT, usually use 英国英语通常用 tap
