faint

1  to suddenly become unconscious for a short time 晕倒，昏厥 SYN pass out

 Several fans fainted in the blazing heat.

几个球迷在炽热高温下晕过去了。

2  I nearly/almost fainted spoken used to say that you were very surprised by something 我差点昏过去〔表示很惊讶〕

 I nearly fainted when they told me the price.

他们告诉我价格时，我差点昏过去。
