Inclined means that you're likely to do something, you're leaning toward doing it, or you do it habitually.

I'm inclined to trust him.

我比较信任他。

We can go for a walk, if you feel so inclined.

你要是愿意的话，咱们去散散步吧。

I'm inclined to believe he's innocent.

我颇以为他是无辜的。

Generally speaking, I'm inclined to agree with you.

总的说来，我比较同意你的意见。

Louise is very musically inclined.

路易丝很有音乐天赋。
