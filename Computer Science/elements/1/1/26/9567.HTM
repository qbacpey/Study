A modem (which comes from modulator/demodulator) is a device that originally took the digital signals from a computer and enabled them to be sent over a voice line where a modem on the other side changed them back to digital signals. But people call the device that hooks their local network to their ISP a modem even when no voice line is involved. ie a cable modem.

本质上来说，现在通常也把将家庭电脑和 ISP 连接起来的设备称为modem，尽管这个东西已然不再涉及任何数字信号和模拟信号之间的转换了
