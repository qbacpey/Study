1  extremely serious or terrible 极其严重的，极可怕的

 warnings of dire consequences that often don’t come true

对严重后果的警告，尽管这些后果常常不会出现

 The country is in dire need of food aid.

这个国家急需食品援助。

 The situation looked dire.

情况看来非常严重。

2  be in dire straits to be in an extremely difficult or serious situation 处于岌岌可危的境地

 Everyone agrees the sport is in dire straits.

人人都认为这个体育项目已处于岌岌可危的境地。

3  dire warning/prediction/forecast a warning about something terrible that will happen in the future 可怕的警告/预测/预言

 Last night there were dire warnings of civil war.

昨晚传来关于内战的可怕警告。
