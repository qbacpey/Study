Similarly, flicker can refer to a sudden and short-lived feeling. A child opening a present might have a flicker of excitement before realizing that the box contains only tube socks and underwear.（指情感稍纵即逝）

(of a light or flame) burn or shine unsteadily （指灯光或火焰）闪烁，摇曳：

All the lights flickered for a moment.所有的灯都闪了一会儿。
