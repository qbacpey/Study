Title: stub

 stub

n

short end piece or stump remaining from a pencil, cigarette or similarly-shaped object; butt （铅笔、香烟等物的）残段，残端；铅笔头；烟蒂：

The crayon had been worn down to a stub.这枝蜡笔用得只剩一小段了。

The dog only has a stub of a taila very short one.这条狗的尾巴很短。

counterfoil 票根；存根：

fill in a cheque stub填写支票的存根。

stub

v

(-bb-)

TnTn.prstub sth (against/on sth) strike (esp one's toe) accidentally against sth hard 使（尤指脚趾）碰到某物：

I've stubbed my toe on a rock.我的脚趾碰到石头上了。

stub sth out

extinguish (esp a cigarette) by pressing it against sth hard （在硬物上）碾灭（尤指香烟）。
