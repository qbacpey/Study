Upset has multiple senses. It generally refers to something that has been knocked over, turned upside down, or disturbed. Just as your coffee cup can be upset — knocked over or disturbed –- so can your emotions –- and your stomach.

撞到、翻转、搞得乱七八糟

情绪乱七八糟、胃部难受

Cheese often upsets her/her stomach.

她吃乳酪后常常感到不舒服［肠胃不适］.

A large wave upset the boat.

一个巨浪打翻了船。
