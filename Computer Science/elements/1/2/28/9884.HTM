Havoc is a noun that means devastation or total mayhem. Havoc often wreaked by hurricanes, angry mobs, plundering Vikings, and wild parties that get out of control.

The floods created havoc.洪水造成了巨大的灾害。

make havoc of sth; play/wreak havoc with sth

damage or upset sth 破坏或扰乱某事物：

The bad weather played havoc with our plans.天气恶劣把我们的计划打乱了。
