grant :

an amount of money given to someone, especially by the government, for a particular purpose 〔尤指政府等发给的〕补助金，拨款

 The university gets a government grant.

这所大学获得政府拨款。

 Anyone wishing to apply for a grant should write to the Treasurer.

凡希望申请补助者，请致函财务主管。

grant of

 a grant of £50,000

五万英镑的补助金

grant from

 These studios are funded by a grant from the Kress Foundation.

这些工作室由克雷斯基金会拨款资助。
