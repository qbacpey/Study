When you give someone a good, long look, you regard them.

as regards sb/sth

concerning or connected with sb/sth 关于或至于某人［某事物］:

I have little information as regards his past.我对他过去的情况不太了解。

As regards the second point in your letter...关于你信中的第二点…

regarding

prep

with reference to (sb/sth); concerning 对于（某人［某事物］）；关于；至于：

She said nothing regarding your request.她对你的要求只字不提。
