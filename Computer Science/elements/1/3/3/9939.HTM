Popularity is the quality of being well-liked and having a lot of friends and admirers. Winning a student council election isn't just about popularity— you also have to have good ideas and be willing to work hard.

when something or someone is liked or supported by a lot of people 流行，普及，受欢迎；声望

 The popularity of the Internet has soared.

gain/grow/increase in popularity (=start to be liked by many people) 开始流行

 Country music is growing in popularity.

乡村音乐越来越流行了。
