When a natural disaster causes widespread death and destruction, that's devastation. This is a strong word that suggests serious damage has occurred, leaving almost nothing left.

 

Rob was devastated by the news of her death.

罗布听到她死去的消息悲痛欲绝。（to make someone feel extremely shocked and sad 令〔某人〕极度震惊；使〔某人〕伤心欲绝）

 The city centre was devastated by the bomb.

市中心被这枚炸弹摧毁了。（ to damage something very badly or completely 严重破坏，彻底破坏，摧毁）
