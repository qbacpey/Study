incline sb towards sth persuade sb to do sth; cause a certain tendency in sb; influence sb 说服某人做某事；使某人有某倾向；对某人施加影响：

His love of languages inclined him towards a career as a translator.他对语言的热爱促使他从事翻译工作。

His sincerity inclines me to trust him. 他很真诚，不由得我不信任他。

incline to/towards sth have a physical or mental tendency towards sth （身体上或思想上）有某种倾向；倾向于；易于：

He inclines to laziness.他爱偷懒。

She inclines towards depression.她动不动就情绪低落。
