luxury : Title: luxury

1  [uncountable] very great comfort and pleasure, such as you get from expensive food, beautiful houses, cars etc 奢华，奢侈

in luxury

 She stole to keep her boyfriend in luxury.

她靠行窃让男友享受奢侈的生活。

 He was leading a life of luxury in Australia.

他在澳大利亚过着豪华舒适的生活。

luxury hotel/car/home etc (=expensive and large) 豪华的酒店/汽车/宅邸等

 We stayed in a five-star luxury hotel.

我们住在一家五星级的豪华酒店里。

 The dress is lambswool – pure luxury.

礼服是用羔羊毛制作的——绝对奢华。
