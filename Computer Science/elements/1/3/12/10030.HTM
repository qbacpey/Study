coordinate : coordinate

1  [transitive] to organize an activity so that the people involved in it work well together and achieve a good result 协调〔多人参加的活动〕

 The agencies are working together to co-ordinate policy on food safety.

这些机构在共同合作为食品安全政策做协调工作。

2  [transitive] to make the parts of your body move and work together well 协调，协同〔身体动作〕

 Her movements were beautifully co-ordinated.

她动作协调，显得很优美。

 I couldn’t get my brain to function or coordinate my muscles.

我无法让大脑运作起来协调肌肉的运动。

3  [intransitive, transitive] if clothes, decorations etc coordinate, or if you coordinate them, they look good together because they have similar colours and styles （使）〔衣服、装饰等〕搭配，协调

 Don’t be afraid to mix colours, as long as they co-ordinate.

不要害怕混搭颜色，协调就行。

 You might coordinate your curtains and cushions.

你应该把窗帘和靠垫的颜色搭配好。

coordinate with

 The cooker is green, to co-ordinate with the kitchen.

厨灶是绿色的，是为了和厨房的颜色协调。
