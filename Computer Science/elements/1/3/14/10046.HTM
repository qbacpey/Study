pledge : Title: pledge

n

give a pledge never to reveal the secret 保证决不泄密（solemn promise; vow 誓言；誓约；保证）

thing left with a person to be kept until the giver has done sth promisedpaid a debt 保证物（如为还债的）。

article left with a pawnbroker in exchange for sth, esp money 典当物。

gifts exchanged as a pledge of friendship 交换的象征友谊的礼物（thing given to sb as a sign of friendship, love, etc （表示友情、爱情等的）信物，象征物）

put/hold sth in pledge以［收下］某物作抵押（in/out of pledge left with sb until the giver has paid a debt, etc/no longer left on these conditions 在抵押中［已不在抵押中］）

take sth out of pledge赎回抵押的某物（in/out of pledge left with sb until the giver has paid a debt, etc/no longer left on these conditions 在抵押中［已不在抵押中］）

make a solemn promise never to drink alcohol 发誓戒酒（sign/take the pledge）

You are under pledge of secrecy.你已发过誓要保密（under pledge of sth in the state of having agreed to or promised sth 在誓约或承诺某事的约束下）
