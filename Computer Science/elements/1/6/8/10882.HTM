settle :

settle sth (with sb) make an agreement about sth; arrange sth finally or satisfactorily; deal with sth 对某事物达成一致意见；终于或满意地安排好某事物；解决；处理：

settle a dispute, an argument, an issue, etc解决一争端、争论、问题等

That settles the matter.事情就那样解决了。

Nothing is settled yet.什么也没定下来。

You should settle your affairs (by making a will) before you leave.你应该把你的事情处理好再走（如先写好遗嘱）。

It's time you settled your dispute with him.现在你该跟他把争端解决了。

We have settled that we will leave next week.我们已经安排好下星期走。

Have you settled how it will be done?对于如何处理这件事，你们是否已达成协议了?
