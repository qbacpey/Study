saturation : Title: saturation

1.N-UNCOUNT 不可数名词饱和过程(或状态);饱和 Saturation is the process or state that occurs when a place or thing is filled completely with people or things, so that no more can be added.

Reforms have led to the saturation of the market with goods...

改革已经使得市场上商品饱和。

Road traffic has reached saturation point.

公路交通已达到饱和点。
