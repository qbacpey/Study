Title: promotion

n

Uraising or being raised to a higher rank or position 提升；晋级：

gain/win promotion获得晋级

If you are successful, you can expect promotion.你只要做出成绩，就能指望获得提升。

attribpromotion prospects晋升的可能性。

Cinstance of this 提升；晋升：

The new job is a promotion for her.这份新工作对她来说是一次提升。

Upromotion of sth encouragement or aid to the progress of (a cause) （对某事业的）促进，提倡，赞助：

They worked for the promotion of world peace.他们为促进世界和平而努力。

Uadvertising or other activity intended to increase the sales of a product （为推销商品而作的）广告宣传，推销活动：

She is responsible for sales promotion.她负责推销工作。

Advertising is often the most effective method of promotion.做广告往往是最有效的推销方法。

Cadvertising or publicity campaign for a particular product （某商品的）推销广告或宣传活动：

We are doing a special promotion of our paperback list.我们正在搞推销平装书的特别宣传活动。

promotional-ʃənl; -ʃənl

adj

of or relating to promotion(3b) 推销的；推销广告或宣传的：

a promotional tour by the author作者以推销作品为目的的巡游。
