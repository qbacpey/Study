settle :

settle (on/over sth) come to rest on sth; stay for some time on sth 在某处停歇或停留一时：

Will the snow settle?Will it remain on the ground without melting?这雪呆得住吗? （能落在地上不融化吗? ）

The bird settled on a branch.那只鸟落在树枝上了。

Clouds have settled over the mountain tops.那些云彩在山顶上空停留不散。

The dust had settled on everything.到处都是积尘。

The cold has settled on my chestIt is making me cough, etc.我患感冒已引起胸部不适（咳嗽等）。

A tense silence had settled over the waiting crowd.在等候的人群中有一种紧张的寂静感。
