Title: with respect to something

formal

a) concerning or in relation to something 与某事有关

 the freedom of a property owner to make a contract with respect to his property

业主就其产业缔结合同的自由

 

b) used to introduce a new subject, or to return to one that has already been mentioned 关于某事，谈到某事〔用于另起话题或回到之前的话题〕

 With respect to your request, I am not yet able to agree.

至于你的要求，我还不能同意。
