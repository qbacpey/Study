Title: organic

1  FARMING 农业 relating to farming or gardening methods of growing food without using artificial chemicals, or produced or grown by these methods 不使用化肥的，有机的，绿色的

 Organic farming is better for the environment.

有机耕作对环境更有利。

 organic gardening

有机园艺

organic food/vegetables/milk etc

 The shop sells organic food.

这家商店出售有机食品。

 organic wine

有机葡萄酒

 

2  DEVELOPMENT 发展 change or development which is organic happens in a natural way, without anyone planning it or forcing it to happen 自然发展的

 The company’s path to success was by means of organic growth.

这家公司是通过逐步发展壮大走上成功之路的。

3  LIVING THINGS 生物 living, or produced by or from living things 生物的，有机物的 OPP inorganic

 Adding organic matter such as manure can improve the soil.

施粪肥之类的有机物能够改良土壤。

 Bacteria act on organic waste.

细菌对有机废物产生作用。

4  PART OF STH 某物的部分

an organic system or relationship is one in which the parts or people fit well and in a comfortable way with each other 有机的；关联的；统一的

 an organic relationship between the individual and his community

个体和他所在社区之间不可分割的关系

 They believe in the organic unity of the universe.

他们相信宇宙是一个有机的整体。

5  BODY ORGANS 身体器官 relating to the organs of the body 器官的

 organic diseases

器官疾病

—organically /-kli/ adverb

 organically produced cheese

用有机方法制作的奶酪

 A writer’s style must develop organically.

一个作家的创作风格必定是逐步发展而来的。
