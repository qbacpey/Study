In fact, on UNIX, the C compiler is itself a shell program! The compiler first invokes a process to expand header include files, then a separate process to parse the output, another process to generate (text) assembly code, and yet another to convert assembly into executable machine instructions

如果要给 shell 下一个定义的话，它是一个通过调用其他进程来帮助自己完成任务的程序
