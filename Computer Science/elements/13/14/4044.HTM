巨变;猛增;飞跃；突飞猛进 A quantum leap or quantum jump in something is a very great and sudden increase in its size, amount, or quality.

【搭配模式】：ADJ n

The vaccine represents a quantum leap in healthcare...

这种疫苗的问世是医疗保健领域的重大突破。

The scale of migration took a quantum leap in the early 1970s.

20世纪70年代初，移民的规模骤然扩大。
