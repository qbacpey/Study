Synchronous I/O structure

• After I/O starts, control returns to user program only upon I/O completion.

– Wait instruction idles the process until the next interrupt

– Wait loop – For one process, at most one I/O request is outstanding at a time, no simultaneous I/O processing.

同步 IO 结构只会在 IO 操作完成的时候将控制权返回给用户

 

对于同一个进程来说，如果这个进程采用同步 IO 结构，那么同一时间只有它只有一个 IO 请求正在被处理
