Save three key values. The hardware saves the values of the stack pointer (the x86 esp and ss registers), the execution flags (the x86 eflags register), and the instruction pointer (the x86 eip and cs registers) to internal, temporary hardware registers.

硬件首先会将三个关键值保存到某些临时硬件寄存器中，这是整个上下文切换过程的第二步

上下文切换中三个关键值（按照课本中图自上而下）分别为：用户栈寄存器（SS:ESP）、用户代码寄存器（CS:EIP）和进程状态字寄存器（EFLAGS）
