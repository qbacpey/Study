organic :

4  PART OF STH 某物的部分

an organic system or relationship is one in which the parts or people fit well and in a comfortable way with each other 有机的；关联的；统一的

 an organic relationship between the individual and his community

个体和他所在社区之间不可分割的关系

 They believe in the organic unity of the universe.

他们相信宇宙是一个有机的整体。
