See the word force inside of enforce? It can help you remember that enforce is all about forcing others to follow the rules. The traffic police enforce the driving laws by handing out speeding tickets. Your parents hopefully enforce good behavior by grounding you when you stay out too late.

enforce sth (on sb) force people to obey (a law, etc); make sth effective 强迫人服从（法律等）；使某事物生效；实施；执行：

The police are there to enforce the law.有警方管执法。

make (sth) happen or bring (sth) about by force 迫使（某事）发生：

enforced silence, discipline, idleness被迫的沉默、强迫执行的纪律、被迫的无事可做。

give greater force or strength to (an argument, a belief, etc) 加强（某论点、信念等）：

Have you any statistics that would enforce your argument?你有没有支持自己论点的统计数字?
