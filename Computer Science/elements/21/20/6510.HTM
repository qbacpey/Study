Title: What's the difference between DMA Controller and I/O processor : I/O processor

You can think I/O processor along the lines of DMA approach. The I/O processor, generally used in large computer systems, is a coprocessor which is capable of executing the instructions in addition to transfer of data. By the way, the coprocessor instruction system is different from the CPU.
