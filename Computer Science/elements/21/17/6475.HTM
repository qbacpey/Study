When you talk about resemblances, you're talking about people or things that look alike in some way. Relatives often have resemblances because they share the same genes.

There is a degree of resemblance between the two boys.

这两个男孩有某些相似之处。

Your story bears/has/shows little or no resemblance to the facts.

你说的与事实相去甚远，或谓根本不符。
