Title: hooked

1  curved outwards or shaped like a hook 钩状的

 a hooked nose

鹰钩鼻

2  [not before noun] informal if you are hooked on a drug, you feel a strong need for it and you cannot stop taking it 〔吸毒〕成瘾的 SYN addicted

hooked on

 I know a girl who got hooked on cocaine.

我认识一个染上可卡因毒瘾的姑娘。

3  [not before noun] informal if you are hooked on something, you enjoy it very much and you want to do it as often as possible 对…上瘾的，对…着迷的

hooked on

 I got hooked on TV when I was sick.

我生病时看电视上了瘾。

4. having one or more hooks 带钩的
