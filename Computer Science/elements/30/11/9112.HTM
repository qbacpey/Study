Native ports accept traffic from a single VLAN and are used to link end devices like routers, servers, terminals etc. They do not need to accommodate an additional identification tag as they are involved in exchanging traffic with a single end device. Hence the name, untagged ports.

VLAN 交换机上非中继端口被称为？原生端口（native port）

VLAN 交换机上的原生端口指的是不和其他 VLAN 交换机中继端口相连的端口

VLAN 交换机上用来和诸如路由器、终端设备和服务器相连的端口被称为？原生端口

They are also referred to as access ports.
