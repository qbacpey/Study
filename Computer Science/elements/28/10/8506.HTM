To do something accurately is to do it in a precise, correct, careful manner. A baseball player with a good batting average hits the ball accurately.

an accurate clock, map, weighing machine准确的钟、地图、衡器

accurate statistics, measurements, calculations, etc 准确的统计、测量、计算等

His description was accurate.他的叙述很正确。

careful and exact 精确的；准确的：

take accurate aim 瞄得准

Journalists are not always accurate (in what they write).新闻工作者（的报道）并非一贯准确
