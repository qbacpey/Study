Title: straits

1  (also straits [plural]) a narrow passage of water between two areas of land, usually connecting two seas 海峡；〔狭窄的〕水道

 the Bering Strait

白令海峡

2  be in dire straits to be in a very difficult situation, especially a financial one 陷于极度的困境中〔尤指财务困境〕

 After the war, the country’s economy was in dire straits.

战后该国的经济陷入困境。

 The firm is now in dire financial straits.

该公司目前财务状况极度困难。
