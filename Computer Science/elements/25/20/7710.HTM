escalate : Title: escalate

if fighting, violence, or a bad situation escalates, or if someone escalates it, it becomes much worse (使)〔战斗、暴力事件或不好的情况〕升级；(使)恶化

escalate into

 Her fear was escalating into panic.

她越来越害怕，最后变得惊慌失措。

 The fighting on the border is escalating.

边境地区的战事在不断升级。

 We do not want to escalate the war.

我们不想让战争升级。
