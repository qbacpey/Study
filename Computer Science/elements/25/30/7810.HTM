All models maps user-level threads to kernel-level threads.

无论是，多对一、一对多、一对一、两层模式，本质上来说它们都是将用户线程映射为内核线程

 A kernel thread is similar to a process in a non-threaded (single-threaded) system. The kernel thread is the unit of execution that is scheduled by the kernel to execute on the CPU.

内核线程是一个可被内核调度器调度的执行单元

The term virtual processor is often used instead of kernel thread.

虚拟处理器通常会被用于指代内核线程
