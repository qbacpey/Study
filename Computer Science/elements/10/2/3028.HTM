If you take up organic farming, be sure to use organic fertilizer, not some artificial chemical. Organic describes things that are natural or related to nature.

In common usage, organicis used to mean “healthful” or “close to nature.” It can also describe foods grown without artificial pesticides or fertilizers. In other uses, organicrefers to living things or material that comes from living things. In a more medical sense, organicmeans “relating to the organs of the body,” and in a legal sense, organicdescribes something that is central to an organization or a government.
