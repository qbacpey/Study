It usually reflects the result of arithmetic operations as well as information about restrictions placed on the CPU operation at the current time.

FLAG 寄存器除了能反映最近算术操作的结果，也能反映现在施加在 CPU 上的限制

 

Some of those restrictions may include preventing some interrupts from triggering, prohibition of execution of a class of "privileged" instructions

CPU 上的限制指的是诸如防止某些中断发生，防止某些特权指令被执行等（言下之意就是，内核使用 FLAG 寄存器控制当前进程的优先级）
