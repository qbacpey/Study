Title: saturation

1.N-UNCOUNT 不可数名词饱和过程(或状态);饱和 Saturation is the process or state that occurs when a place or thing is filled completely with people or things, so that no more can be added.

Reforms have led to the saturation of the market with goods...

改革已经使得市场上商品饱和。

Road traffic has reached saturation point.

公路交通已达到饱和点。

2.ADJ 形容词(运动、活动)饱和的，全面的 Saturation is used to describe a campaign or other activity that is carried out very thoroughly, so that nothing is missed.

【搭配模式】：ADJ n

The concept of saturation marketing makes perfect sense...

饱和营销的概念是完全有道理的。

Newspapers, television and radio are all providing saturation coverage.

报纸、电视和电台全都在铺天盖地地报道。

...saturation bombing.

饱和轰炸
