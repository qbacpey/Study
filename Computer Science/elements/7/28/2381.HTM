Title: cease

cease to do something

 He ceased to be a member of the association.

他不再是该协会的会员。（to stop doing something or stop happening 停止，终止，结束 ）

 

 The things people will do for charity never cease to amaze me (=I am always surprised by them).

人们的慈善义举总是让我感到惊讶（to stop doing something or stop happening 停止，终止，结束 ）

 

cease doing something

 the decision to cease using CFCs in packaging

停止在包装材料中使用氯氟烃的决定 （to stop doing something or stop happening 停止，终止，结束 ）

 The rain ceased and the sky cleared.

雨过天晴。 （to stop doing something or stop happening 停止，终止，结束 ）

cease trading/production/operations etc (=stop operating a business) 停业/停止生产/停止经营等（to stop doing something or stop happening 停止，终止，结束 ）

 The company ceased production at their Norwich plant last year.

去年该公司在诺里奇的工厂停止了生产。 （to stop doing something or stop happening 停止，终止，结束 ）

cease fire! (=used to order soldiers to stop shooting) 停止射击！
