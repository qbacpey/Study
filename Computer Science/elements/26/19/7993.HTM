Title: doctor

give medical treatment for (sth) or to (sb) 医治（某病）；为（某人）治病：

doctor a cold, a child医治感冒、给小孩治病。

neuter (a cat, dog, etc) 阉割（猫、狗等）。

add sth harmful to (food or drink) 将有害物质搀入（食物或饮料）中：

They doctored her fruit juice with vodka and she got very drunk.他们在她喝的果汁里搀入了伏特加酒致使她酩酊大醉。

change (sth) in order to deceive 窜改，伪造（某事物）：

doctor the evidence, the accounts, a report伪造证据、窜改帐目、作假报告。
