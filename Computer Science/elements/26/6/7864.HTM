Switch 1 tags all frames it receives from device 1 with VLAN ID 1 because device 1 belongs to VLAN 1, before forwarding the frames on the trunk. Similarly, it tags all frames from device 2 with VLAN ID 2.

Switch 2, on receiving the tagged frames, decapsulates them to check which VLAN they are destined for and forwards them accordingly.
