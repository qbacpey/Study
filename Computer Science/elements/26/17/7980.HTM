In telecommunications and computer networks, a channel access method or multiple access method allows more than two terminals connected to the same transmission medium to transmit over it and to share its capacity.

频道接入方式（Channel access method）或者说多路访问方式（Multiple access method）允许多个终端同时接入到同一个传输媒介当中并在其上传输数据

A channel access method may also be a part of the multiple access protocol and control mechanism, also known as medium access control (MAC).

多路访问方式往往是媒介访问控制（Medium Access Control）中的一个部分，它的目的就是让多个终端共享同一物理媒介

根据实现方式的不同，多路访问协议（Multiple access Protocol）能被分为诸如：频道划分协议、随机访问协议、轮流协议等
