Title: luxury

1  [uncountable] very great comfort and pleasure, such as you get from expensive food, beautiful houses, cars etc 奢华，奢侈

in luxury

 She stole to keep her boyfriend in luxury.

她靠行窃让男友享受奢侈的生活。

 He was leading a life of luxury in Australia.

他在澳大利亚过着豪华舒适的生活。

luxury hotel/car/home etc (=expensive and large) 豪华的酒店/汽车/宅邸等

 We stayed in a five-star luxury hotel.

我们住在一家五星级的豪华酒店里。

 The dress is lambswool – pure luxury.

礼服是用羔羊毛制作的——绝对奢华。

 

2  [countable] something expensive that you do not need, but you buy for pleasure and enjoyment 奢侈品 OPP necessity

 luxuries like chocolate and perfume

巧克力、香水之类的奢侈品

3  afford/have/enjoy the luxury of something to have something that is very pleasant or convenient, that you are not always able to have 难得能享受…的机会

 For the first time in three years, they actually had the luxury of a whole day together.

三年来他们第一次有这样难得的机会，可以一整天都在一起。
