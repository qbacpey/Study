The thread_switch code may seem tricky, since it is called in the context of the old thread and finishes in the context of the new thread.

thread_switch 通过修改寄存器和栈指针，成功使得处理器以旧线程的身份进入 thread_switch 函数，以新线程的身份离开 thread_switch 函数
